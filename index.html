About Me

Currently pursuing a Master’s degree in UI/UX Design at Gujarat University in India, I aim to leverage my creative skills to design innovative and intuitive user experiences. Passionate about design, I seek to contribute to impactful projects by blending aesthetics with functionality, while continuing to enhance my skills in a dynamic professional environment.

What I Do

  • UI/UX Design

    I design modern, intuitive, and visually captivating user interfaces tailored to both web and mobile applications. My expertise includes developing user flows, wireframes, and interactive prototypes to ensure seamless and engaging user experiences.

  • Front-End Development

    I transform creative designs into responsive and high-quality websites using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. I also utilize no-code platforms like Adalo and WordPress for fast and efficient development, collaborating closely with back-end developers to ensure flawless functionality.

  • Branding & Visual Design

    I create impactful branding and visual identities that align with your business goals. From crafting professional-grade logos to designing marketing materials, I bring ideas to life using tools like Photoshop, Illustrator, and Canva, ensuring consistent and effective brand communication.

  • Prototyping & Interactive Solutions

    I specialize in building interactive prototypes that validate concepts with real users. Using tools such as Figma, Adobe XD, and Sketch, I transform ideas into immersive, user-centric designs that enhance engagement and bring your vision to life.
